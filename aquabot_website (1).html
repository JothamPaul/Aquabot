AQUABOT

Autonomous Water Quality Monitoring & Purification

A solar-powered autonomous boat that navigates Chennai's polluted lakes, maps water quality in real time via GPS, and purifies water through activated carbon filtration and UV-C sterilisation.

📡 CONNECT ESP32
Enter the IP address shown in Arduino IDE Serial Monitor when the ESP32 boots. The dashboard will display live sensor data automatically once connected.
The ESP32 prints its IP address to Serial Monitor at 115200 baud on startup.
